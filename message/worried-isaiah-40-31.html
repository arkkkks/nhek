Verse
Meaning: those who wait on the Lord receive renewed strength to keep moving forward.
Isaiah 40:31
Message
As a high-achieving student, worry can grow from heavy expectations, deadlines, and fear of getting things wrong, but this truth shows that those who wait on the Lord receive renewed strength to keep moving forward. Let this shape your mindset so you can prepare with focus, pray with honesty, work step by step, and stay emotionally steady, because your growth is built through faithful habits, wise choices, and trust in God rather than panic, comparison, or pressure.
