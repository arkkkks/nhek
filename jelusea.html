Story about stress

"Burnout by: Amelia Nagoski"

Amelia Nagoski was working as a choral conductor — a demanding job she loved. She cared deeply about her students and pushed herself relentlessly.

One day, she collapsed from stress and was rushed to the hospital.

Doctors told her she was suffering from severe burnout. Her body was shutting down.

Recovery taught her that stress must be released, not ignored. Rest, movement, and boundaries saved her.
